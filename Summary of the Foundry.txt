🏭 The Foundry – Outbound Infrastructure

A modular, API-first system for provisioning, testing, warming, sending, replying, prospecting, and monitoring. Each Forge is a specialized tool; together they form a closed-loop outbound machine with no SaaS tax, no hidden throttles, and no ESP red flags.

🛠️ ConfigForge — Provisioning & Setup
Automates new domain onboarding
Creates mailboxes (Emily, Hannah, Megan, Lauren, etc.)
Configures DNS (SPF, DKIM, DMARC, MX, tracking, TNS, etc.)
Registers domains with ESPs (SparkPost) + mailbox provider (Migadu)
Ensures compliance & warm-up readiness

🧪 TestForge — Preflight & QA Lab
Spam filter testing (SpamAssassin, Gmail/Outlook classifiers)
Email rendering previews (Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail, desktop/mobile)
A/B testing harness for subject lines, CTAs, personalization
Technical validation (DKIM/SPF/DMARC alignment, tracking links)
Sandbox sends to seed lists before live campaigns
Acts as a quality gate before campaigns enter WarmForge/SendForge

🔥 WarmForge — Reputation Builder
Private warm-up pool under your control
Realistic automated reply threads
Domain/IP health monitoring before outreach

⚡ SendForge — Cold Outreach Engine
Fine-grained throttling (X emails every Y minutes)
Rotate across domains, mailboxes, and ESPs
ESP-aware sending rules (skip Outlook, throttle Gmail, delay Yahoo)
Unified inbox (“Unibox”) for replies

💬 ReplyForge — Conversation Manager
Centralized inbox for all replies
Auto-classify responses (positive, referral, unsubscribe, OOO, etc.)
AI-draft replies + smart autoresponders (Calendly links, polite deferrals)
CRM + Slack/Telegram integration for hot-lead alerts

📋 ListForge — Prospecting Hub
Scrape leads (LinkedIn, Crunchbase, Reddit, niche directories)
Enrich with verified emails and firmographics
Push directly into SendForge queue
GPT-powered personalization snippets (icebreakers, first lines)

📊 TrackForge — Deliverability Radar
Inbox placement testing (seed accounts across Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo)
SPF/DKIM/DMARC + blacklist monitoring
ESP health scoring → auto-pause or reroute sends
Cross-suite analytics dashboard

🔁 The Foundry Flywheel

ConfigForge provisions new domains + mailboxes.
TestForge validates technical + creative setup.
WarmForge builds domain/mailbox reputation.
ListForge fuels the system with enriched prospects.
SendForge executes cold outreach with precision.
ReplyForge manages and automates inbound replies.
TrackForge monitors deliverability and performance.

Together, the seven forges form a complete outbound control stack — from setup → validation → warm-up → sending → replies → analytics — all under your ownership and control.
