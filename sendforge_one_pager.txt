🛠 How It Works

Queue contacts in Google Sheets or Airtable (with personalization fields)

n8n scheduler trickles them out (X per Y minutes)

SparkPost API handles delivery

IMAP listeners collect replies and log them centrally

Your Unibox shows the full picture, all in one place
