💬 ReplyForge — Engagement & Response Automation

Purpose:
ReplyForge is the conversation manager inside The Foundry. It centralizes all replies from SendForge campaigns into a single Unibox, classifies them automatically, and triggers downstream workflows.

Core Functions:

Unibox: Unified inbox across all domains and mailboxes

Classification: Auto-sort replies (positive, negative, referral, unsubscribe, OOO, bounce)

Automation: Trigger CRM syncs, Slack/Telegram alerts, autoresponders

AI Assist: Draft replies, generate polite deferrals, insert scheduling links

Analytics: Feed reply data back into TrackForge for deliverability + health scoring

Why It Matters:

Operators get one dashboard for all conversations

No lost leads — hot replies are surfaced instantly

Reply signals strengthen domain reputation (with WarmForge + TrackForge feedback loop)

Enables scaling cold outreach without drowning in inbox chaos
